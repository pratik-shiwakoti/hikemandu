Our Story

Discover the journey that led us to become Nepal's premier trekking and adventure company

Born from Passion, Driven by Adventure

Hikemandu was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate mountaineers and local guides who shared a common dream: to showcase the breathtaking beauty of Nepal's mountains while promoting sustainable tourism.

2015

The Beginning

Started as a small group of local guides offering day hikes around Kathmandu Valley. Our founder, Rajesh Sherpa, had a vision to create authentic mountain experiences.

2017

Growing Community

Expanded our services to include multi-day treks and established partnerships with local communities. We began our commitment to sustainable tourism practices.

2019

Digital Innovation

Launched our digital platform to make mountain adventures accessible to everyone. Introduced online booking and real-time trail information.

2021

Sustainable Focus

Became a certified sustainable tourism operator. Launched our "Leave No Trace" initiative and community development programs.

2024

Leading the Way

Today, we're proud to be one of Nepal's most trusted adventure companies, having guided over 10,000 trekkers safely through the Himalayas.

What Drives Us

Authentic Experiences

We believe in providing genuine, unfiltered mountain experiences that connect you with Nepal's natural beauty and rich culture.

Environmental Responsibility

Every trek we organize follows strict environmental guidelines to preserve the pristine beauty of our mountains for future generations.

Community Support

We work closely with local communities, ensuring that tourism benefits the people who call these mountains home.

Safety First

Your safety is our top priority. All our guides are certified and our equipment meets international safety standards.
