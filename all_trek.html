Best Trekking Seasons

Spring (March-May): Clear mountain views, rhododendrons in bloom, moderate temperatures.

Autumn (September-November): Crystal clear skies, stable weather, perfect visibility.

Winter (December-February): Lower altitude treks, fewer crowds, crisp air.

Difficulty Levels

Easy: 3-6 hours daily, well-marked trails, basic fitness required.

Moderate: 5-7 hours daily, some steep sections, good fitness needed.

Challenging: 6-8 hours daily, high altitude, excellent fitness required.

Extreme: 8+ hours daily, technical sections, expert level fitness.
