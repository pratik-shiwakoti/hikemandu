CYCLING ESSENTIALS

What We Provide & What to Bring

Everything you need to know for a safe and enjoyable cycling experience

What We Provide:

  • High-quality mountain bikes and city bikes
  • Safety helmets and protective gear
  • Professional cycling guides
  • Basic repair tools and spare parts
  • First aid kit and emergency support
  • Route maps and cultural information

What to Bring:

  • Comfortable cycling clothes
  • Water bottle (minimum 1 liter)
  • Sunscreen and sunglasses
  • Light snacks and energy bars
  • Camera for capturing memories
  • Valid ID and emergency contacts

Safety Guidelines:

  • Always wear provided helmets
  • Follow traffic rules and guide instructions
  • Stay hydrated throughout the tour
  • Inform guide of any health conditions
  • Maintain safe distance from other cyclists
  • Use hand signals for turns and stops
