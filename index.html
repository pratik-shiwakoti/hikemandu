About Hikemandu

Founded by passionate trekkers and outdoor enthusiasts, Hikemandu is dedicated to showcasing the natural beauty of Nepal's diverse landscapes and providing unforgettable trekking experiences.

Our team of experienced guides and photographers have explored countless trails across the Himalayas, capturing breathtaking moments and creating safe, memorable adventures for trekkers of all experience levels.

Whether you're planning your first trek or are a seasoned hiker looking for new challenges, Hikemandu is your gateway to Nepal's majestic mountains.
