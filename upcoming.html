Event Details

Get ready for the most thrilling event of the year! We're hosting a special Hike with lots of exciting activities like hiking, group adventures.

Hike Info

  • Route: Banepa - Chandeshwori temple - Raut Gaun - Nange dada - Sanga/Nala Hike
  • Distance: 12-14 km (Refrence: Google Maps)
  • Date: 5 Baishak, 2082
  • Duration: 5-6 hours (Approx)
  • Difficulty: Easy-Moderate (Best for Beginners)
  • Altitude: 1400m-1900m (From sea Level)
  • Places of Attraction: Falcha, Chandeshwori thuosand steps, Gorakkali Mandir and more.
  • Meeting Time: 10:00 AM (We hope everyone will be on time, we won't wait if you are late)

  • Meeting Location:KU Bus Stop, Dhulikhel

  • Transportation: Public Bus (Up to KU Bus Stop)
  • Weather: Sunny with a chance of rain
  • Cost: NPR 200-500 per person
  • Organizer: Hikemandu
  • Contact: 9813092717

Hiking Essentials

  • Clothing: Simple & Comfortable clothing is essential to adapt to changing weather conditions.
  • Footwear: Light, Comfortable, and Waterproof Trekking Shoes/Boots. (Choose footwear with good ankle support and traction for varied terrain)
  • Water: Carry plenty of water for the duration of Hike. (Minimum 2 Litres)
  • Food: Homemade Foods (Chocolates/Juice/Fruits/Dry fruits/ Dehydrated meals). Please avoid bringing junk food, carbonated drinks, and alcohol.
  • Fitness: Ensure you are in good physical condition and a Positive Attitude before embarking on a hike.
  • Accessories: Cap/Hat, Sunglass, Gloves,Raincoat/Umbrella, Medicine (For Emergency), trekking pole(optional)
